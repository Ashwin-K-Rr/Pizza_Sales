📊 Pizza Sales Analysis

This project analyzes pizza sales data using MySQL to uncover key business insights across sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

🔍 Key Insights
Identified top-performing pizzas driving the highest orders and revenue
Highlighted low-performing items for potential menu optimization
Analyzed category-wise sales trends to understand customer preferences
Explored monthly and weekday order patterns to detect demand trends
Compared revenue vs quantity sold to evaluate pricing impact
📈 Business Impact
Helps optimize menu by focusing on high-demand pizzas
Supports decision-making for promotions and pricing strategies
Enables better planning using time-based sales trends
Improves overall revenue and operational efficiency
